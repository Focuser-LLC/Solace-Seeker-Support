## Privacy Policy

*Last updated June 2026*

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what Solace Seeker does — and does not — do with your information.

### 1. Data stays on your device
Saved Solace Spots and your app preferences are stored locally on your device only. We do not operate servers that collect, sync, or back up this data.

### 2. Sensor data
Microphone audio is analyzed live, on-device, to estimate sound levels. Audio is never recorded, stored, or transmitted. Motion, magnetometer, and light-sensor values are processed in real time to compute disturbance scores and are not uploaded.

### 3. No accounts, no tracking
Solace Seeker does not require an account and does not include third-party advertising or analytics trackers that identify you personally.

### 4. Deleting your data
You can permanently remove all saved spots and reset your preferences at any time from Settings → Data → Delete all data. Removing the app also deletes its local data.

### 5. Children
Solace Seeker is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

For privacy questions, reach out to us at [info@focuserllc.com](mailto:info@focuserllc.com).
